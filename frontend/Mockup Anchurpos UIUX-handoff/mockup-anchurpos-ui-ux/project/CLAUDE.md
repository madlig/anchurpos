# AnchurPOS UI/UX Mockup — Project Notes

## Design System
- **Primary Pink**: #E85D8C (buttons, accents, active states)
- **Base Background**: #FCABB4 (phone frame & desktop content backgrounds)
- **Success**: #16A34A (green)
- **Warning**: #D97706 (amber)
- **Error**: #DC2626 (red)
- **Font**: Plus Jakarta Sans (400/500/600/700/800)
- **Style**: Clean & professional with pink brand identity from Instagram

## Completed Screens (in AnchurPOS Mockup.dc.html)
1. **Login** — Simple & cute, email + password, full pink background
2. **Manager Dashboard** — Greeting, omzet card with progress bar, stats, recent orders
3. **Manager Kasir/POS** — Search, category tabs, product grid, FAB cart
4. **Manager Pesanan** — Status tabs (Semua/Pending/Proses/Selesai), order cards
5. **Manager Inventori** — Low stock alert, stock items with progress bars
6. **Manager Lainnya** — Profile card, settings menu, logout
7. **Crew Absensi** — Status card, large clock-in button, daily log
8. **Crew Produksi** — Summary stats, production items with progress + tambah button
9. **Owner Dashboard** — Multi-outlet overview, total revenue, pending approvals, per-outlet cards
10. **Form Publik** — WhatsApp order form, customer info, product stepper, order summary
11. **Desktop Dashboard** — Sidebar nav, stat cards grid, orders table
